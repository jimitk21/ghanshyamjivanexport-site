About GhanshyamJivan Exports

GhanshyamJivan Exports is a leading agricultural product export company based in India, specializing in premium quality groundnuts, onions, peanuts, and sesame seeds. With years of experience in the international market, we have established ourselves as a reliable supplier of high-quality agricultural products to clients worldwide.

Our commitment to quality, timely delivery, and customer satisfaction has earned us the trust of importers across the globe. We work directly with farmers and implement strict quality control measures to ensure our products meet international standards.

Our Story

Founded in 2025, GhanshyamJivan Exports was built on a foundation of agricultural expertise and a deep-rooted commitment to quality. Before we were exporters, we were farmers—understanding the land, the seasons, and the dedication required to cultivate the best produce.

Our journey into the export industry began generations ago when our ancestors, through local trade networks, unknowingly became part of a global supply chain. With this legacy, we have embraced our role in connecting premium Indian agricultural products—such as groundnuts, onions, peanuts, and sesame seeds—to international markets.

Today, GhanshyamJivan Exports proudly serves clients across the USA, Europe, Asia, and beyond, delivering excellence with every shipment. Our reputation for reliability, ethical trade practices, and unwavering commitment to customer satisfaction has made us a trusted name in the industry.
